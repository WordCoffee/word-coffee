Our Story: Brewing Breakthroughs.

Co-founded by Tyler Edwards and Tyrone, Word Coffee was born from a shared belief in the transformative power of words. Tyler's journey through military and investment banking, coupled with overcoming anxiety, fuels the brand's dedication to self-improvement and courageous action.

Tyrone brings his passion for creative expression, emotional impact, and business acumen from building previous brands, ensuring Word Coffee is not just inspiring, but also effectively delivered.

Together, we created Word Coffee: a movement built on the conviction that the right words can truly fuel breakthrough action and authentic growth.
